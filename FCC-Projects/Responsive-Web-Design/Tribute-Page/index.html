Brief Synopsis from Wikipedia

Born August 24, 1963, Hideo Kojima is a Japanese video game designer, screenwriter, director, and game producer. Regarded as an auteur of video games, he founded Kojima Productions in 2005, after starting at Konami Digital Entertainment where he rose to vice president in 2011. Since the end of 2015, Kojima has been an independent game developer. His most notable video game is the Metal Gear series of stealth games, as well as the adventure games Snatcher and Policenauts. He has also produced Zone of the Enders, Boktai, and Castlevania: Lords of Shadow.
